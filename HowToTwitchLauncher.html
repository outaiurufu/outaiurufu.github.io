How to Install a Custom Pack into the Curse Launcher

  1. Download the Custom Pack
  2. Open your Curse Launcher to the Minecraft Page
  3. Click on the "Create Custom Profile" Button at the top
  4. Click on "Import" in the text above the Profile Name Text Box
  5. Find the downloaded custom pack on your Hard Drive and click on Open
  6. Wait for the install to finish
  7. DONE
